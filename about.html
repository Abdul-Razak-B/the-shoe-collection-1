THE VISIONARY

Founded by Abdul Razak Bhutto, a computer science visionary from Daharki, The Shoe Collection was born from a singular obsession: merging futuristic comfort engineering with timeless aesthetics.

With a background in Computer Science and an eye for architectural design, Abdul Razak approached footwear through the lens of performance optimization. He saw an opportunity to bring high-tech manufacturing and premium bio-materials to the local market and beyond.

"Our headquarters in Daharki serves as our creative hub, where we prototype every sole, test every stitch, and ensure that every pair that leaves our doors is a testament to quality," says Abdul Razak.
